About

Stocking, processing,
and delivering steel products

In the steel service center industry, The Company is known for its commitment to value-added service and solid reputation for on-time delivery. Our greatest depth and expertise lies in the stocking, processing, and on-time delivery of carbon, alloy and stainless steels. This includes plate, hot rolled and cold finished bars, structurals, tubing, and expanded metals.

Services

Continuing the legacy of excellence

Our success lies in the commitment to delivering consistent and uncompromising quality in both products and services. To achieve this, it is equipped with some of the most advanced and efficient slitting & shearing machines and facilities. It is also well-staffed with highly skilled and> competent personnel at all levels. Complemented by the support in both supply and technical assistance from some of the world's finest steel producers and service centres through its affiliations, Steel Service Centre is able to cater to the most stringent and exacting requirements of its clients.
